Research and development

These are times of crisis. Around the world there are stories of battles for mouth masks, multiple patients on air ventilators due to lack of supply and PPE shortages restricting the sick seeing their relatives. Motivated to make a difference, the PplCert team is researching how to help fight COVID-19 using digital solutions.

The PpICert team aim to help with changing the:

  • Abundance of product offers
  • Lack of product certification
  • Unstable supply & resource market
  • Changing logistical barriers
  • Short decision-making timeframe
  • Information silos
